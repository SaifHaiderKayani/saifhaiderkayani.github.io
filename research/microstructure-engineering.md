<h1>Microstructure Engineering</h1>

<h3>Overview</h3>

<p align="justify">Microstructural refinement in Al-Si-based alloys is critical for enhancing mechanical properties such as strength, toughness, and elastic modulus. The addition of inoculants plays a pivotal role in modifying the solidification process and refining the microstructure which not only promotes the formation of fine, uniform particles but also enhances the overall elastic modulus. This refinement leads to better control over phase distribution and enhances the alloyâ€™s mechanical properties, including wear and fatigue resistance. The effectiveness of inoculants in controlling particle size and promoting desirable microstructural features underscores their importance in optimizing the performance of Al-Si alloys.</p>
