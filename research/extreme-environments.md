# Materials for Extreme Environments

## Overview
Research focused on sustainable alloy development, recycling-aware alloy chemistry, and property retention in secondary aluminum systems.

## Key Topics
- Low-carbon alloy design
- Scrap-tolerant aluminum alloys
- Impurity-tolerant microstructures

## Methods
- Alloy thermodynamics
- EBSD, TEM, APT
- Mechanical and corrosion testing
